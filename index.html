Who We Are

Aditya Exima is a sustainable export company specializing in premium areca leaf–based products such as plates, bowls, and tableware. We are committed to delivering eco-friendly alternatives that combine natural aesthetics with global quality standards.

Areca-based products are 100% biodegradable and compostable, making them an ideal replacement for plastic and paper disposables. By using areca products, we reduce plastic pollution, support sustainable farming, and promote a healthier planet—without compromising on strength, hygiene, or design.
